Here are the basic description of each massage therapy treatment:

- Swedish massage is a massage treatment method that uses variations of long gliding strokes, kneading, and friction methods on surface muscles. Strokes used in Swedish massage normally goes with the direction of blood flow toward the heart. Swedish massage is commonly availed by people who wants to experience relaxation, enhance circulation and range of motion, along with relieve muscle stress.

- Deep tissue massage is a massage treatment technique targeted at reaching the muscles method below the surface area level. Chronic patterns of muscular tension usually occurs at the deep connective tissues and this is the area targeted by deep tissue massage. With making use of slow strokes, direct and firm pressure, along with friction techniques directed throughout the location of the body experiencing chronic muscle pain, deep tissue massage works in alleviating chronic muscular tension and discomfort.

- Sports massage is a massage treatment method that uses massage strokes comparable to those utilized throughout a Swedish massage and deep tissue massage. The difference is that sports massage is specially adjusted to handle the results of athletic efficiency on the body, in addition to the requirements of professional athletes relating to training, performing, and healing from injury.
